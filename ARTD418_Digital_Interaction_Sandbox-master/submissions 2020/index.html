Hypermedia Narratives

ARTD_418 Advanced Digital Interaction. University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

Juan Salamanca Ph.D. Jan-May, 2020

In this 16-week class, the students conceptualized and prototyped hypermedia narratives for the web. They explored multimedia languages that articulate text, images, video, animation, sound and mouse interactions to narrate stories of their choice. The production of this body of work demanded technical skills in video and sound editing, animation, HTML and CSS and the use of javaScript libraries.
